GLOBAL JUSTICE LOVE AND PEACE EVENT

Cylsys Software were the Tech partners for The Global Justice, Love & Peace Summit, a Star-studded event held on the 12th & 13th April 2025 in Dubai. It was a gathering of unprecedented magnitude dedicated to the ideals of Justice, Love & Peace.

Lokesh Ji Muni Acharya

  • Acharya Lokesh Muni is a renowned Jain spiritual leader and Global Peace Ambassador. He is the founder of Ahimsa Vishwa Bharti and the World Peace Centre, organizations dedicated to promoting non-violence, global peace, and interfaith harmony. participated in the Global Justice, Love & Peace Summit 2025 held in Dubai on April 12–13, 2025. Known for his lifelong dedication to non-violence, interfaith harmony, and social reform, he graced the summit with his presence and insights .

Baba Ramdev

  • Baba Ramdev, a globally renowned Yoga Guru and co-founder of Patanjali Ayurved, was featured in the Forbes India Celebrity 100 list and recognized by UNESCO for reviving ancient Indian practices. He is a strong advocate for the Swadeshi movement and Indian sciences. At the Global Justice, Love & Peace Summit 2025 in Dubai, he shared his insights on promoting global justice, love, and peace through yoga, pranayama, and holistic wellness.

Urvashi Rautela

  • Urvashi Rautela, a renowned Indian actress and model, was crowned Miss Diva Universe 2015 and represented India at Miss Universe 2015. She actively supports women empowerment, education, and skill development, while also engaging in health awareness campaigns. Known for her role in Bollywood's "Sanam Re," Urvashi is also recognized for her philanthropic work and strong social media presence. She participated in the Global Justice, Love & Peace Summit 2025 in Dubai, sharing her insights on promoting peace and empowerment.

Kailash Satyarthi

  • Kailash Satyarthi was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 2014, emphasizing that global peace and justice are not possible without children's freedom. He is a passionate advocate for child rights and the founder of the Bachpan Bachao Andolan (Save Childhood Movement), which has rescued over 100,000 children from child labour, trafficking, and slavery.participated in the Global Justice, Love & Peace Summit 2025 held in Dubai on April 12–13, 2025.

Bhai Saheb Satpal Singh Khalsa

  • Bhai Saheb Satpal Singh Khalsa, based in the USA, serves as the Ambassador of Sikh Dharma in the Western Hemisphere. He is the Chairman of the Guru Ram Das Sikh Mission and Secretary of International Affairs for Sikh Dharma International. He also serves as the Director of Miri Piri Academy in Amritsar, promoting Sikh education and spiritual values. participated in the Global Justice, Love & Peace Summit 2025 held in Dubai on April 12-13, 2025.

Dr., Deepa Malik

  • Dr. Deepa Malik is a renowned Indian para-athlete and motivational speaker. She won the silver medal at the 2016 Rio Paralympics, becoming the first Indian woman to do so. Awarded the Arjuna Award, Padma Shri, and Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna, she has earned over 100 medals in national and international events. Dr. Malik holds four Limca World Records and serves as the President of the Paralympic Committee of India. She is also associated with Smile Train India, contributing to its mission.

Sanjeev Kapoor

  • Sanjeev Kapoor is a world-renowned Indian chef and entrepreneur, widely recognized for his contributions to the culinary world. He actively participates in charitable initiatives focused on combating hunger and promoting nutrition. Kapoor campaigns to provide meals to underprivileged communities and supports organizations dedicated to food security.participated in the Global Justice, Love & Peace Summit 2025 held in Dubai on April 12–13, 2025.

General, Bikram Singh

  • General Bikram Singh is the former Chief of the Indian Army and served as the UN Force Commander in Congo. Awarded the Param Vishisht Seva Medal, he led counter-insurgency operations in Jammu and Kashmir and promoted the modernization of the Indian Army. He is also an advocate for the welfare of soldiers and veterans. General Singh participated in the Global Justice, Love & Peace Summit 2025 in Dubai, contributing to discussions on peace and justice.

Esha deol

  • Esha Deol is a renowned Indian actress, classical dancer, and producer. She produced the national award-winning film “Ek Dua” and is a trained Odissi dancer and athlete. Esha gained recognition for her roles in movies like Dhoom, Kaal, and Yuva. She participated in the Global Justice, Love & Peace Summit 2025 held in Dubai on April 12-13, 2025.
